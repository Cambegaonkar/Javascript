Class Details:

  1. Tai JiFit Flow – Mondays 10:30 am – 11:15 am It combines the best elements of fitness, meditation and the ancient martial art of Taiji (Tai Chi)
  2. Total Body Blast – Mondays 5:30 pm – 6:15 pm Combines resistance training and high-intensity aerobics. This class is easy-to-follow and targets strength building as well as muscular endurance.
  3. Stretch & Strength – Mondays 6:15 pm – 7:00 pm This workout aims to bring the body into balance through functional exercises. It stretches the tight and strengthens the weak to bring the body to a stronger, longer version of itself.
  4. Total Fit Seniors – Tuesdays & Thursdays 9:00 am – 10:00 am Includes cardiovascular and muscle conditioning exercises for older adults and younger participants wanting a moderately paced workout.
  5. NRG!-MMCC (Metabolic, Mobility, Conditioning, Core) - Tuesdays & Thursdays 6:15 pm – 7:15 pm This class will have 20 minutes of high intensity interval training, 20 minutes of muscle endurance and strength conditioning (using body weight and/or equipment) and 20 minutes of foam rolling, and dynamic and static stretching.
  6. Intro Yoga – Wednesdays 9:00 am – 10:00 am Exploring awareness and bodily postures, breathe work and relaxation techniques.
